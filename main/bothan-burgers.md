# Bothan Burgers

## Serves
4

## Ingredients
 - 500g mince beef
 - 1 medium onion
 - 1 medium egg
 - 1 handful of bread crumbs
 - 0.5 tablespoon crushed coriander seeds
 - pinch crushed cumin seeds
 - 1 teaspoon mustard
 - salt and pepper

## Method
 - add everything to a bowl and mix
 - fry/bbq
