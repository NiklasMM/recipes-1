# Stir Fry Sauce

## Serves
2 (obviously add stir fry stuff to it)

## Ingredients
 - rice wine
 - rice vinegar
 - oyster sauce
 - ketchup
 - worcestershire sauce
 - light soy sauce

## Methods
 - equal amounts ketchup and oyster sauce (about a table spoon of each)
 - lots of soy sauce to make the volume
 - a splash of wine, vinegar, worcestershire
