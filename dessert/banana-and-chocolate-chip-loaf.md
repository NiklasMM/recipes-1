# Banana and Chocolate Chip Loaf

## Serves
1 loaf

## Ingredients
 - 2 very ripe banana
 - 1 tablespoon milk
 - 50g butter
 - 75g plain flour
 - 75g caster sugar
 - 0.5 teaspoon bicarb
 - 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
 - 1 egg
 - 50g chocolate chips

## Method
 - mash bananas
 - cream the butter
 - add sugar to butter
 - whisk in the egg and flour
 - add the milk and mix
 - add chocolate and bananas
 - bake at 160C for 40min
